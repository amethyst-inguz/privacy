# Privacy Policy for Pure Count
**Last updated: [28-01-2026]**
This Privacy Policy describes how **Pure Count** ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information.
## 1. Information Collection and Use
**Pure Count** is an offline-first application. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, store, or share your personal data (such as your name, email address, or phone number) on our servers.
All your counter data is stored locally on your device.
## 2. Third-Party Services (Ads)
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements in our app. Google AdMob may collect and use data to show you personalized ads.
*   **Google Mobile Ads SDK**: This service may collect data such as device identifiers, location data, and other usage information to provide relevant advertisements.
*   For more information on how Google manages data, please visit [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).
## 3. Data Storage
All data created within the app (e.g., counters, goals) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information will be permanently deleted. We do not have access to this data and cannot restore it for you.
